Privacy Policy

Last Updated — May 2026

Steadfast Care & Stewardship

At Still Waters Tech, I believe that technology should serve you quietly, honestly, and without intrusion. Respecting your privacy is a foundational aspect of professional stewardship and honest craftsmanship. This policy explains what happens when you visit this website.

No Data Collection

This website does not collect personal identifiers directly. There are no contact forms, registration systems, email newsletter sign-ups, or user account fields.

No Tracking or Cookies

I do not utilize tracking cookies, advertising trackers, or web analytics platforms (such as Google Analytics or Meta Pixels) that profile your behavior across the web.

Standard Server Logging & CDNs

Like virtually all websites on the Internet, this site relies on standard technical infrastructure that processes temporary network requests:

  • Server Logs: The platform hosting this static website automatically collects standard network information, such as your IP address, browser user-agent string, referrer URL, and timestamps. This is a technical requirement to safely serve the website and detect malicious activities.
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs): This site loads third-party libraries (including Tailwind CSS, GSAP, and Google Fonts) from established, secure CDNs. These services may temporarily observe your IP address to deliver the requested files to your browser.

Direct Communications

When you initiate contact using the email link (`mailto:`) provided on this site, we receive your email address and any details you choose to include in your message. This communication is strictly used to address your business inquiry. Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Inquiries & Contact

If you have questions or concerns regarding this policy, feel free to write directly to contact@stillwaterstech.org.
